The Best Movies According to Josie

My top movies of all time in no specific order

Becoming Jane

I love historical fiction and the acting in this movie makes it timeless to me.

Slumdog Millionaire

This movie pulls at my heartstrings and makes me feel so much. I still know the dance to Jai Ho:)

Peanut Butter Falcon

This movie is so heart-warming and was written/produced(?) by the same people who made Little Miss Sunshine - another favorite of mine.

The Fall Guy

I've watched this movie three times since it came out a few months ago and would happily watch it again. It's witty, cute, exciting, gives you a thrill, makes you care, and shines light on an interesting subject. The acting is amazing, which doesn't suprise me based on the cast.

Disturbia

I can't have a favorite movies of all time list and not put this one on here. It wouldn't be fair to all the friends i've made watch it with me since Junior High.

How to lose a guy in 10 days

I am a big fan of Kate Hudson and Matthew McConoghey rom-coms. They just go together.
